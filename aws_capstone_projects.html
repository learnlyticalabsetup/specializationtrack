üõí Capstone Project 1: Global E-Commerce Platform

Design and implement a globally distributed, highly available e-commerce platform

Duration: 6 Days | Complexity: Enterprise Level

Business Scenario

GlobalShop Inc. is launching an international e-commerce platform targeting 50 million customers across 25 countries. The platform must handle Black Friday traffic spikes (100x normal load), provide sub-200ms response times globally, support multiple currencies and languages, ensure 99.99% availability, and comply with GDPR, PCI DSS, and local data sovereignty laws.

Key Business Requirements:

  • Support 10,000 concurrent transactions per second during peak
  • Global deployment across 6 AWS regions
  • Real-time inventory management across 500+ warehouses
  • Advanced analytics and personalization engine
  • Multi-tenant architecture supporting marketplace vendors
  • Disaster recovery with 15-minute RTO and 5-minute RPO

The platform must achieve SOC 2 Type II compliance, PCI DSS Level 1 certification, maintain 99.999% uptime, process 50,000 transactions per second, and provide real-time fraud detection with zero tolerance for data breaches.

Key Business Requirements:

  • Real-time transaction processing with immediate settlement
  • Advanced fraud detection with ML-powered risk scoring
  • Multi-channel customer experience (web, mobile, API)
  • Regulatory reporting and audit trail capabilities
  • Open banking API compliance (PSD2, Open Banking Initiative)
  • Zero-downtime deployments with immediate rollback capabilities
